# LLM Primer

**Systematic Learning of Large Language Models: From Theory to Practice**

A bilingual, multi-tiered, community-driven LLM learning knowledge base covering foundational concepts, engineering practices, cutting-edge papers, and interactive demos.

## What / Who / How Long

- **30 minutes**: Build mental models through intuitive explanations, understand core LLM concepts
- **3 hours**: Complete engineering tier, master practical implementation and performance optimization
- **On-demand deep dive**: Track paper lineage, open questions, and frontier developments in research tier
